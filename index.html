About me

Hello...

My name is Lee. I just graduated from Michigan State University, have done a few things, and am looking for a job. I can write well, design visuals, and help you or your company grow with digital marketing.

...is it me you're looking for?

I always saw the draw of talented creatives. But not in the Yoko Ono way. In the small stand-up comedy room kind of way. To me, creativity is about drawing people in, keeping them interested, and leaving them with something to take away. It's mutually beneficial, and the creative gets the benefit of admiration, or trust, or love.

If you were looking for a personality summation: I make a lot of painful jokes, I know a handful of words in French, and I wrote the movie Xanadu (1980). That's about all that you need to know.
